About

The Pacific Northwest Garden Club was established in 1959 in Sequim, Washington. Our goal is to spread joy to all thorugh the beauty of growing. From fruits, vegetables, flowers, succulents, perrenials and many more we are here to spread knowledge and appreciation to the gardening community and beyond. Every member of the Pacific Northwest Garden Club is welcomed with open arms and those from all backgrounds and walks-of-life are encouraged. The PNGC strives to be a haven for every and any gardener, veterined and newbies to grow (pun inteneded) in their abilities and knowledge while esablishing relationships with fellow growers.
